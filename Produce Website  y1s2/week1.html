Planning Defining goals and strategy

Focus: This week concentrates on the crucial planning phase - the foundation of any successful website.

üéØ Define Your Website Goal

Before building a website, you must clearly understand why you need one.

Your website goal determines the design, features, and content strategy you'll implement.
Website Type Primary Goal Key Features
E-commerce Sell products online Shopping cart, payment gateway, product catalog
Portfolio Showcase work/skills Gallery, case studies, resume
Blog Share information/ideas Article listings, comments, RSS feed
Business/Corporate Build credibility & generate leads About us, services, contact form

üè¢ What Are Your Business Goals?

Your business goals directly influence your website's purpose and design.

Business Goal Website Strategy
Increase Sales E-commerce platform with conversion optimization
Generate Leads Lead capture forms, call-to-actions, landing pages
Build Brand Awareness Content marketing, social proof, storytelling
Provide Customer Support Knowledge base, FAQs, chatbot, ticketing system
Educate Audience Blog, tutorials, resources library, webinars
Remember: Your website should align with your overall business strategy and objectives.

üë• Who Is Your Target Audience?

Understanding your target audience is critical for creating a website that resonates and converts.

Key Questions to Answer:

Category Questions to Consider
Demographics Age, gender, location, income level, education
Psychographics Interests, values, lifestyle, personality traits
Behavior Online habits, device usage, preferred platforms
Pain Points Challenges they face, problems they need solved
Goals What they want to achieve, their aspirations

üìä Audience Research Methods

Research Method Description Benefits
Surveys & Questionnaires Collect direct feedback from potential users Quantifiable data, specific insights
Analytics Review Analyze existing website/social media data Real behavior patterns, conversion data
Competitor Analysis Study similar websites in your industry Best practices, gap identification
User Interviews One-on-one conversations with target users Deep qualitative insights, pain points
Social Listening Monitor social media conversations Trending topics, common questions
Pro Tip: Use multiple research methods for a comprehensive understanding of your audience.

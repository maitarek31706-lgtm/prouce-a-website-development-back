To launch a website, you need three essential components: a domain name, web hosting, and a platform to build your site.
Component Purpose Example
Domain Name Your website's address on the internet www.yourbusiness.com
Web Hosting Server space where your website files are stored Shared, VPS, Dedicated, Cloud
Website Builder Tool to create and design your website WordPress, Wix, Squarespace

üî§ What is a Domain Name?

A domain name is your website's unique address on the internet - it's what people type in their browser to find you.
Domain Part Description Example
Subdomain Optional prefix before domain blog.yoursite.com
Second-Level Domain Your unique name yoursite
Top-Level Domain (TLD) Domain extension .com, .org, .net, .edu
Tip: Choose a domain that is short, memorable, and relevant to your brand or business.

üñ•Ô∏è What is Web Hosting?

Web hosting is a service that stores your website files on a server and makes them accessible on the internet 24/7.
Feature Description
Storage Space Amount of disk space for your files
Bandwidth Data transfer capacity for visitors
Uptime Percentage of time server is operational
Security SSL certificates, backups, malware protection
Support Technical assistance availability

Dedicated Hosting Large websites, high traffic Full control, maximum performance Very expensive, requires expertise
Cloud Hosting Scalable needs, reliability Scalable, reliable, flexible Variable pricing, can be complex

Choose your platform based on your technical skills, budget, and website requirements.
Your Situation Recommended Solution Why?

Key Takeaway: There's no one-size-fits-all solution. Choose based on your specific needs, skills, and budget.
